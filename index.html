Switch

Welcome to switch

The Goal of the Game is to get rid of all your cards first


Rules

  1. Each Player is Dealt 7 Cards
  2. One non trick card Is dealt face up in the middle
  3. You MUST play any card that is the same Suite or value as the face up card
  4. if you cannot play you must pick a card from the pile to add to your hand
  5. if you pick up a playable card you can play it before your turn is over
  6. You can play connected run cards i.e 3,4,5 or 5,4,3 of the same suit
  7. You can play connected number cards at the same time i.e 5,5,5
  8. You can combine all of these rules to remove all your cards i.e play 9,10,jack it back -> jack it back => Ace to 6-H,6-C
  9. runs can end on a trick card to carry on your shot . i.e 9,10,jack it back

Trick cards

  1. Ace - Is played to check the current face up card to any other card and suit or the same suite
  2. You Cannot Ace it to your last card
  3. Jack - iss played to jack it back to you and you can play any other jack, suited card or ace
  4. 8 - playing an 8 is asking to other opponent to play an 8 or wait
  5. 2 - similar to an 8 but you ask your opponent 2 or pick up 2, if you opponent fails to play a 2 they have to pick up 2 cards, if they play a 2 you have to play a 2 or pick up 4
  6. If your turn finished on your shot i.e a jack and you cant play you must pick a card
